Biryani

Prep Time: 1 hr | Serves: 6

  • Rice, Meat, Yogurt, Onions
  • Spices, Garlic, Ginger, Oil
    1. Soak rice and marinate meat with yogurt, spices, garlic, and ginger.
    2. Fry onions in oil until golden and set aside.
    3. Cook the marinated meat until tender.
    4. Partially cook the rice.
    5. Layer meat and rice, top with fried onions, then cook on low heat until done.
    6. Serve hot.
Ice Cream

Prep Time: 45 mins | Serves: 4

  • Milk, Cream, Sugar, Vanilla Extract
  • Egg Yolks (optional)
    1. Mix milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, and egg yolks (if using) until smooth.
    2. Heat gently while stirring until slightly thickened.
    3. Cool the mixture, then freeze and churn until creamy.
    4. Serve or store in freezer.
